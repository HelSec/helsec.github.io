Welcome to the CitySec Xmas Party

This event is powered by HelSec & Accenture.

We are gathering together to celebrate the past year and upcoming holidays for a bit different sort of meet-up, a huge party. We will begin with presenting visiting CitySec chapters and few words from the hosts, but after that we let music do the talking.

Coat charge has been covered & there will be some amount of food & beverages available for the hungriest & thirstiest, but when the freebies run out, they run out.

If your plans change, please remember to cancel your ticket so that someone on the wait list can grab your spot.
