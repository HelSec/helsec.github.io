Frequently asked questions

Helsec

  • HelSec is for everyone interested in learning more about all things cyber, and networking with other like-minded people.

Membership

How can I join HelSec?

  • You can join HelSec by filling the membership application on Flomembers

How can I volunteer at HelSec?

  • We open the volunteer application once a year, usually in January. The application period lasts for 2–3 weeks, after which we try to promptly select volunteers for the current year.

Can I join the board of HelSec?

  • A new board is selected in the statutory Fall meeting. Members are notified in advance and encouraged to get in touch if they are interested in joining the board. The working language of the board is English.

My company/employer wants to support HelSec, how can we do it?

  • HelSec welcomes support members. Price of the support membership is currently 1500€ per year. Please ask your company to contact us at contact@helsec.fi

How do I update my member information?

  • You can update your information in FloMembers.
